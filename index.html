About Me

Hey! My name is Soren, and I'm a full-stack web developer located in Seattle, Washington. I have just recently broken into the web development field and have a strong passion for creating elegant and highly-functional web applications. I discovered my passion for web development while teaching myself HTML, CSS, and JavaScript through my own research and study online. I decided to pursue the field full time, and enrolled in an immersive, full time web development course through General Assembly. Through this course I have developed my skills with NodeJS, Ruby on Rails, jQuery, Angular, Postgress, MongoDB, and a variety of other technologies.

Having been trained as a full stack developer, I enjoy all aspects of the development process, however I take particular pride in making the front end of a project responsive, highly functional, and easy for the user to interact with. I strongly enjoy working with jQuery, AJAX, Angular, CSS, and UI Libraries such as Bootstrap, Foundation, and Materialize.

Specific skills and technologies aside, at this point in my Career, my greatest passion is still for learning and continuing to grow as a developer. In addition to honing the skills I already have, I hope to continue expanding my skillset to include technogies and frameworks that I don't yet know. Ultimately I want to continue growing as a developer so that I can do more for myself and those I work with.
